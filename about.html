About Us

Who are we?

We are a Kenyan based online marketplace, that provides a platform for people to buy and sell quality clothes and accessories.

Our Mission

To create an efficient trading marketplace that offers high quality products for purchase, not only in Kenya but worldwide.

What we do

* We make high quality items available to the public at an affordable cost.

* We help upcoming designers, designers and traders sell and showcase their products for sale on our platform.

* We give potenial customers a wide variety of goods to choose from according to their needs.
